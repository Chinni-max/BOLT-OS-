How it works

Three steps to a seamless life

1
You express intent
Speak or type anything — "book my usual cab", "draft a reply to Priya", "order dinner before my meeting". BOLT understands natural language perfectly.
2
Agents take over
BOLT deploys the right combination of AI agents — they call APIs, make bookings, draft content, set alarms, and coordinate across your entire digital life.
3
You just live
Everything is done. BOLT sends you a quiet summary. No app-switching. No forms. No confirmations. Just the result, delivered silently and instantly.
